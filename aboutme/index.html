    Why Did I set Up This Blog?

    As I previously stated, I am interested in aspects of lifestyle journalism, one being fashion. When thinking of fashion journalism, a typography blog may not be what first springs to mind. But, like fashion, typography is rich in culture and history: it's historically significant, dating back to ancient Egyptian carvings; culturally significant, as aplhabets, symbols, fonts and more are all unique and have different meanings; and socially significant as it defines the way we see brands. Therefore, I set up this blog becuase I wanted to explore the significance of typography within branding because it's so important for a journalist to understand the significance of a brand.

    Why Did I Set Up This Blog?

    I wanted to create a blog that could casually dicuss the importance of typography within branding. My site is great for young literature, language, media, business, marketing and art students as typography is incorporated into all of these careers, and it uses simple language that can be understood by all. I want to inspire young people by showing that anyone can set up a blog and have some fun writing articles in a casual style!
